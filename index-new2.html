ni laughed

i was drinking a bottled tea
and smoking a cigarette with Ni
at a little bench near the window that we sometimes used as a table.

I was nodding off
and the cigarette had burnt a small hole near the neck of the tea bottle.

You could see perfectly into the bottle.

I picked up the bottle
and slowly turned it sideways...,
to see the stream of tea pour from the small hole......nothing came out.

the cigarette
had not burned all the way through,
only the colored plastic label that wrapped the bottle had been melted.

I looked at Ni who had been nodding off and said, "look a tea hole" - Ni laughed and laid on my lap.
ni laughed   2007 Taipei, Taiwan
