Affinity Solutions

Creative Director

Marketing solutions for retailers, financial institutions, publishers, and processors, based on unique actionable analytic insights.


Affinity Solutions designed and managed loyalty programs, retailer campaigns, and offer distribution. With the ability to pair "the right offer with the right consumer" based on analytics, including retail transactions and spend data. As Creative Director at Affinity Solutions, I oversaw all creative items for both Affinity Solutions and all of its active/potential clients.

Including formulating design/UI/UX solutions, new product development, migrating existing client platforms to HTML5 and responsive mobile solutions.

Lead and managed the Creative Team in design, functionality, and copy writing for visual communications; including managing the team's workflow and asset delivery.

  • Lead cross-functional creative sessions for project kick-offs, and problem solving efforts
  • Problem solve UI/UX issues while acting as the internal SME
  • Oversaw all HTML, CSS, Jquery and ADA development
  • Managed multiple projects and the associated resources, from concept through completion
  • Ensured quality and efficiency, while managing schedules of both long/short-term projects
  • Developed new delivery methods/processes for the loyalty programs, retailer campaigns and email marketing
  • Supported sales initiatives throughout the company including RFP materials
  • Supervised and inspired the Creative Team including furthering the teams knowledge base and career paths
  • Managed the workflow and end-product delivery of the Creative Team
