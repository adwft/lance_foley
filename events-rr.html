Robb Report

Experiential Design & Events

Robb Report - widely regarded as the single most influential journal of luxury living, brings the pages of the magazine to life by creating unique and extraordinary experiences. Showcasing the newest in what matters most to its sophisticated audience through exclusive Signature Events, partnerships with luxury brands, and new product launches celebrating the very best in the worlds of design, engineering, craftsmanship, culture, and cuisine.

Musings

A brand new speaker series celebrating the launch of Muse by Robb Report. Part luxury, part thought leadership, Musings offers a unique live forum in which influential women—trailblazers, tastemakers, idealists, and disruptors—share their views and values on topics ranging from style, design, the arts, travel, wellness, wealth management, and philanthropy.

Car of the Year

Robb Report’s annual Car of the Year experience puts VIP attendees in the driver’s seat during the ultimate event that celebrates luxury, performance, automotive design, and engineering. During this spirited competition, the world’s greatest automotive marques present their flagship offerings for the coming model year, offering the privilege of stepping in as a Robb Report automotive editor for the day. Guests are able to test-drive and evaluate up to 13 of the most extraordinary upcoming luxury and performance vehicles – to earn the title “Robb Report Car of the Year.”

Morgan Stanley - An Evening at the Nat Sherman Townhouse

A partnered event where guests were invited to network with the Millman Group at Morgan Stanley. The night included the Luminor Due collection, a new chapter in the history of Panerai luxury watches, live demonstrations of traditional leather craftsmanship with luxury leather goods atelier Slightly Alabama, a selection of the finest cigars from legendary Nat Sherman, and a preview of Robb Report’s November Investor’s Index, which offered an exclusive look at the collector market and an insider’s perspective on the global auction industry.
